Welcome to the GradMate

We are GradMate that brings together the brightest minds from around the nation to support and inspire each other, and to help solve some of the Cadets most pressing problems

What we do

The GradMate is a network of alumni, entrepreneurs, and executives who have worked at some of the most successful technology companies in the nation . We are committed to helping each other succeed in building the next generation of great companies.

Values

Forward thinking

We encourage our members to think big and take risks. We believe that the only way to predict the future is to invent it.

Authenticity

We value honesty and transparency. We believe that the best decisions are made when people are open and authentic.

Curiosity

We believe that the pursuit of knowledge and understanding is a lifelong journey. We encourage our members to ask questions and seek out new experiences.

Diversity

We believe that diverse perspectives lead to better outcomes. We strive to create an inclusive environment where everyone feels valued and respected.

Impact driven

We are committed to making a positive impact on the world. We encourage our members to use their skills and resources to address important challenges.

Resilience

We understand that failure is a natural part of the innovation process. We encourage our members to learn from their mistakes and keep moving forward.
